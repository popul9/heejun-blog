The fourth track on the remake album. The tempo on this one is something else, I can't believe how perfectly it's calibrated. If you listen to Busy Boy (track 3) with its fast tempo and then jump into this one, you're left wondering whether it's slow or fast, and before you know it you're already hooked. That "Sorry.." of yours, keeps skipping past me 😒.
